Usage Meter Widget for BigPond

This is a BigPond usage meter dashboard widget for Mac OS X. The widget monitors how much you have downloaded and uploaded (your usage) and the percent of the month or billing cycle. It updates once a minute when visible.

The widget works on Leopard and Snow Leopard.
