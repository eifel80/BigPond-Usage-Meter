Usage Meter Menu Bar for BigPond

This is a bigpond usage meter menu bar item for Mac OS X. It sits up in the top right. It monitors how much you have downloaded and uploaded (your usage) and the percent of the month or billing cycle.

It works on Leopard and Snow Leopard.
